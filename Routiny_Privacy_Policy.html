Routiny privacy policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the application Routiny (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by GGApps. The Application is a task organizer that allows users to add recurring and non-recurring tasks and keep up with them

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application doesn't collect information about the user, but some third party services like Google AdMob may collect some information, and the privacy policy for their services will be listed below in third parties sectoin

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. But third party services like admob may share location with advertisers

What permissions are required by the application?

Write to Calender

we use the calender in your system and Google Calender if you are using it, to serve you with notifications(reminders) of your tasks which is a part of the application functionality, and of course you can deny the permission to access the calender from permissions on your device, if you don't want the calender to be involved in the application, but no notifications of your tasks will be recived
